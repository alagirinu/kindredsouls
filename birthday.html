A Letter,
on your birthday

❤️

You came into my life like the first kiss of a human love — quiet, unexpected, and then impossible to stop thinking about.

I have seen you love music the way most people breathe — like it is not optional, like the world makes more sense when there is a song playing. And slowly, you taught me to hear things I never noticed before.

I have watched your face when you eat chocolate. That particular expression — that one — is one of my favourite things in the world. Do not let anyone ever tell you to eat less of it.

I know you want to go everywhere. You have a kind of hunger for the world that I deeply admire. Every place you have ever talked about visiting, I want to take you there. Every single one.

You love truly. You feel deeply. You express little — and yet somehow the people who love you can always feel it. I feel it every day.

Happy 32nd birthday. You deserve every good thing this world has. Starting with today.

— I may not say this every day but you mean everything to me ❤️
